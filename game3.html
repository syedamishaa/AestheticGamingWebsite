PUBG Mobile

Overview

PUBG Mobile is a battle royale game where 100 players fight to be the last one standing. Players can team up, loot weapons, vehicles, and supplies while surviving intense combat across massive maps.

Key Features

  • 🪂 Battle Royale Mode
  • 🔫 Realistic Weapons & Vehicles
  • 🌍 Massive Maps & Dynamic Weather
  • 🤝 Team Play & Strategy

Platforms

Android, iOS

Release Date

March 19, 2018
