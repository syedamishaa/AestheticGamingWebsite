Celestial Quest

Overview

Celestial Quest is an epic space adventure game where players explore distant galaxies, battle alien creatures, and uncover ancient mysteries. Players can upgrade their ships, craft advanced technology, and forge alliances across the universe.

Key Features

🚀 Explore vast galaxies
🛠 Upgrade spaceships and technology
👽 Encounter alien civilizations
🎮 Single-player & multiplayer modes

Platforms

Available on PC, PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch.

Release Date

March 2025
