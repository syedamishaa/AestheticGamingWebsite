Free Fire

Overview

Free Fire is a fast-paced battle royale game where 50 players compete on a remote island, collecting weapons and resources to be the last one standing.

Key Features

  • 🔥 50 Players per Match
  • 🔫 Weapons, Vehicles & Gear
  • 🌍 Diverse Maps & Safe Zones
  • 🤝 Team Up with Friends

Platforms

Android, iOS

Release Date

December 4, 2017
