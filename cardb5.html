BioShock

Overview

BioShock is a first-person shooter set in the underwater city of Rapture. Players navigate through a dystopian world filled with genetically modified citizens, powerful weapons, and plasmid abilities.

Key Features

  • 🔫 First-Person Shooter
  • 🌊 Explore Underwater City Rapture
  • 🧬 Use Plasmids for Superhuman Abilities
  • 🎯 Strategic Combat and Resource Management

Platforms

PC, PS3, PS4, Xbox 360, Xbox One

Release Date

August 21, 2007
