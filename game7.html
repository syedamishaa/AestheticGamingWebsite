Subway Surfers

Overview

Subway Surfers is an endless runner mobile game where players take the role of young graffiti artists running from a grumpy inspector and his dog, dodging trains and obstacles while collecting coins and power-ups.

Key Features

  • 🏃‍♂ Endless Running Gameplay
  • 🚆 Dodge Trains and Obstacles
  • 💰 Collect Coins and Power-Ups
  • 🌍 Colorful, Vibrant Environments

Platforms

iOS, Android

Release Date

May 24, 2012
