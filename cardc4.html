Crash Bandicoot 4

Overview

Crash Bandicoot 4: It's About Time is a platformer featuring Crash and Coco as they battle the evil Quantum Masks. Run, jump, spin, and collect Wumpa fruit through colorful, challenging levels.

Key Features

🌀 Fun platforming
👾 Colorful levels
🎯 Collectibles
👥 Multiplayer mini-games

Platforms

PC, PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch

Release Date

October 2020
